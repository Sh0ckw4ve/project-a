What's TalentEd?

The project is called Talented, an online social media platform with the objective of sharing your talents, users will be able to upload images-photos demonstrating their skills, if another user views any of your uploads they can "talent" you. Talents are the amount of times other users have "talented" an upload of yours. Talenters are the total amount of users that "talented" an upload of yours. (not followers) Talenteds are the amount of talentes you've given to other users. Followers, those who follow you. Followed, those whom you follow. The Talent Categories for now are: Arts, Music, Performance/Acting, Coding, DIY Items, Cooking, Sports, eSports. Each user will choose the categories or category when creating their account. In future versions you will be able to choose a category example, sports, you upload your stuff related to sports, you can view other users sports posts, then you can browse the art area for example, but you cannot post for the art area, every day users with most Talents from each category will earn "tokens" which wil be used for buying custom wallpapers, profile styles, animated profile images, then there is Talented InvestOn area, which is an area where investors can define an amount of Talents and view users from selected category that have that amount of Talents or more.
